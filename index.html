Life of a Styracosaurus

The Styracosaurus is a genus of herbivorous ceratopsian dinosaur from the Cretaceous period. It is known for its impressive array of horns and frills, which likely served both defensive and display purposes. Styracosaurus lived approximately 75 million years ago and was primarily found in what is now North America.
